🔍 Diabetes Predictor – User Guide

This application estimates your risk of diabetes. Please input the following values:

  • Pregnancies: Number of times pregnant (e.g., 0–15)
  • Glucose: Blood glucose level (e.g., 70–200)
  • Blood Pressure: Diastolic pressure in mm Hg (e.g., 60–130)
  • Skin Thickness: Triceps skinfold in mm (e.g., 10–60)
  • Insulin: Serum insulin in μU/ml (e.g., 15–300)
  • BMI: Body Mass Index (e.g., 18.0–50.0)
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function (DPF): Hereditary risk score (e.g., 0.1–2.5)
  • Age: In years (e.g., 21–80)

All fields are required. Please enter numeric values only. Data is not stored or shared beyond your session.
